Homemade Pancakes

Description

These homemade pancakes are light, fluffy, and incredibly easy to make from scratch. Perfect for a quick breakfast or a lazy Sunday brunch, this recipe uses basic ingredients you probably already have in your pantry.

Ingredients

  • Flour: 1 ½ cups of all-purpose flour.
  • Baking powder: 3 ½ teaspoons to make the pancakes rise.
  • Salt: A pinch (1 teaspoon) for flavor balance.
  • Sugar: 1 tablespoon for a hint of sweetness.
  • Milk: 1 ¼ cups of milk (whole milk works best).
  • Egg: 1 large egg, beaten.
  • Butter: 3 tablespoons of melted butter.

Steps

  1. Mix the dry ingredients: In a bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. Combine the wet ingredients: In another bowl, mix the milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Combine and whisk: Slowly add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and whisk until just combined. Avoid overmixing.
  4. Cook the pancakes: Heat a lightly greased skillet or griddle over medium heat. Pour ¼ cup of batter per pancake and cook until bubbles form. Flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
  5. Serve: Serve warm with syrup, butter, or your favorite toppings.

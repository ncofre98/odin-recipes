Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Spaghetti Bolognese is a classic Italian pasta dish with a rich and hearty meat sauce. This recipe combines ground beef, aromatic vegetables, and flavorful tomato sauce for an unforgettable meal.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti: 12 ounces of dried spaghetti.
  • Ground beef: 1 pound of lean ground beef.
  • Vegetables: 1 onion, 2 carrots, and 2 celery stalks, finely diced.
  • Tomato sauce: 1 can (14 ounces) of tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes.
  • Garlic: 2 cloves, minced.
  • Olive oil: 2 tablespoons for cooking.
  • Herbs: 1 teaspoon dried oregano and a handful of fresh basil leaves.
  • Salt and pepper: To taste.

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta: Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Saut√© the vegetables: Heat olive oil in a skillet and cook the onion, carrots, and celery until softened.
  3. Cook the meat: Add the ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it up as it cooks.
  4. Add the sauce: Stir in the garlic, tomato sauce, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  5. Assemble: Serve the Bolognese sauce over the spaghetti and garnish with fresh basil.
